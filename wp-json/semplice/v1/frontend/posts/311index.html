YouTube Mockup Tool<\/span><\/h2>

Research \/ User Testing \/ UX \/ UI Design<\/span><\/p><\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>

In 2018 Google created a new division called 'Create with Google'. Create with Google's purpose is to create a community to inspire and educate creatives on the potential of Google's large spread of platforms. A huge part of Create with Googles existence is the creation and supply of useful tools. One such tool is the YouTube Mockup Tool, aimed towards Creatives in the Videography and Advertising Industry.<\/span><\/p><\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\t<\/div><\/div><\/div>\r\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/section>\t\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t\t

They are essentially trimming down their 1.5 min ad into 30 seconds, without looking at how the ad format works, then wondering why the conversions are so low.\u201d<\/span><\/h4>
